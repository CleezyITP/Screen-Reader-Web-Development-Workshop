Day 2 Activity

Create a single page site using a heading, list and link. You can come up with your own idea or choose one of four ideas:

  1. List the buildings you go to at O&P
  2. Write out step by step directions to get to the Blind annex
  3. Create a recipe
  4. Describe your personal history, arranged by important life events (use an ordered list)

Create a single web page for your choice. Please refer to the HTML Element Glossary to help you choose and use elements!
